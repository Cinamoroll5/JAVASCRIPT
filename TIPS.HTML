🌍 How to Save Marine Ecosystems

  • Turn off taps when brushing teeth.
  • Take shorter showers.
  • Fix leaking pipes and taps.
  • Reduce plastic use.
  • Recycle bottles and containers.
  • Never throw trash in rivers or oceans.
  • Protect coral reefs.
  • Join beach cleanups.
  • Save water when washing dishes.
  • Educate others about ocean protection.
